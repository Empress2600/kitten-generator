A Standard Apart.

At Of The Empress, we believe a Bengal should be more than beautiful — it should be singular.

Our program is built on lineage, structure, and the kind of wild expression that cannot be manufactured. Each pairing is chosen with precision: high-contrast rosettes, glittered coats, athletic build, and a temperament that is both confident and deeply present.

Raised within our home from birth. Health-tested. TICA-registered. Selected.

We are particular about where our Bengals go — because extraordinary things belong in extraordinary hands.

Our Sovereigns

The Foundation of our Lineage

Ivan

King • Silver Bengal · Male

A commanding Silver Bengal King, Ivan is the sire behind Achilles and Cobalt — proof of his extraordinary genetic depth. His silver coat, powerful structure, and bold expression are hallmarks of the Empress line.

Maximillion

Cashmere King • Cashmere Bengal · Male

Maximillion is a rare Cashmere Bengal — the long-haired expression of the breed — and one of the most visually stunning males in our program. His silky, flowing coat and wild expression pass down effortlessly to his offspring, including Cobalt and Mykonos.

Katrin

Foundation Queen • Silver Spotted Tabby · Female

An extraordinary producer, Katrin is the dam behind Achilles and Cobalt — two of our most rare and sought-after kittens. Her lineage anchors the Empress program with depth, genetic diversity, and consistent quality.

Kora

Queen of the Cattery • Brown Spotted Tabby · Female

Kora carries herself with the quiet confidence of a true Empress. Elegant in structure and generous in temperament, she is a cornerstone of our breeding program and a beloved presence in our home.

Bella

Queen of the Cattery • Bengal · Female

Bella is as beautiful as her name suggests — expressive, affectionate, and breathtaking in structure. She brings warmth and wild elegance to every litter she produces under the Empress banner.

Blue

Queen of the Cattery • Charcoal Blue Spotted Tabby · Female

Blue is a rare and striking Charcoal Blue Spotted Tabby — her cool, slate-toned coat and vivid green eyes make her one of the most distinctive queens in the cattery. She is the dam of Mykonos, passing her exceptional color genetics to every litter she produces.

A Cashmere is a long-haired Bengal — same wild Asian Leopard Cat heritage, same rosettes and temperament, but with a silky, semi-long coat that carries the pattern beautifully. TICA recently recognized it as its own breed. The long-hair trait is fully recessive, meaning both parents must carry the gene — which makes true Cashmeres genuinely rare. We are developing our Cashmere program and will announce availability when the time is right. Reach out if it interests you.
